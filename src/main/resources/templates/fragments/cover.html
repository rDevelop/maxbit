Welcome

MaxBit is a user-friendly web interface to the ItBit exchange. Fully functional for trading and account management. The backend technology can be integrated into other systems. Enjoy this demo and if you're only viewing as a guest, the pages will be read only and trading is not available.

Features

Get ticker info, the full order book, and recent trades for the specified market. Place, get, and cancel an order. Create fiat and cryptocurrency withdrawls and deposits. Create wallets. Get trades, funding, and balance history for a wallet. Transfer funds between your wallets and your itBit account.
